A small studio where clay and flour share the same shelves.

Wilde Made is a new brand rooted on California's Central Coast. It begins with ceramics — slow, quiet vessels made one at a time — and grows to sourdough: naturally leavened loaves pulled from the oven on weekend mornings.

Every piece leaves here having been touched by hand. Nothing rushed, nothing mass-produced — only things made with the kind of patience that pink dawn deserves.

More to come, as the shelves fill.
